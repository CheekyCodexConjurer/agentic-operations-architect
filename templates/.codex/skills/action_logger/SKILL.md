## Purpose
Record what changed, why, and how it was verified.

## Steps
1. Add an entry to `ACTION_LOG.md`.
2. Mirror the entry in `ACTION_LOG.jsonl`.
3. Include commands and evidence.

## Guardrails
- Keep entries brief and factual.
